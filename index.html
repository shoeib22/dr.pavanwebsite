Clinical Expertise

Specialized Treatments

Using state-of-the-art technology and minimally invasive techniques to treat complex neurological conditions.

Minimally Invasive (Endoscopy) Spine Surgery

Advanced endoscopic techniques (Key Hole Surgery) for spine procedures, ensuring smaller incisions, less pain, and faster recovery.

Neuro Oncology

Comprehensive management of Brain & Spinal Tumors, including benign and malignant lesions like gliomas and meningiomas.

Neuro Trauma

Emergency Craniotomy and rapid 24/7 surgical intervention for traumatic brain injuries, blood clots, and spinal cord injuries.

Treatment for Back Pain

Specialized care for chronic back pain, sciatica, and spinal pathology using both conservative management and surgical solutions.

Management of Headache

Detailed diagnostic evaluation and advanced treatment protocols for chronic headaches, migraines, and neuralgias.

Facial Nerve Preservation

Utilization of Intraoperative Neuromonitoring (IONM) to preserve facial function during complex CP angle tumor surgeries.
